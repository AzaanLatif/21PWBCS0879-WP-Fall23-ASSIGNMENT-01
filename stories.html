Baleja

Baleja Meadows is a lush green meadow in the Siren Valley near Mansehra, that is surrounded by towering mountains and a crystal-clear stream that flows through it. The meadow is dotted with wildflowers, and the fresh mountain air is invigorating. It is a popular destination for nature lovers, hikers, and campers who want to enjoy the scenic beauty and peaceful surroundings of the valley.

Mussa Ka Musalla

Musa ka Musalla (Urdu: موسیٰ کا مصلیٰ transl. Moses' mat) is a peak standing at an altitude of about 4,060 metres (13,320 ft) at the junction of Siran and Kaghan Valleys in Himalayas.[1] It is situated 150 kilometres (93 mi) north of the city of Abbottabad in Balakot Tehsil of Mansehra District.[2]

Manda Gucha

Mandagucha is a village located in Siran Valley, Mansehra District, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan. The village is on the route to one of the highest hilltops of the Himalaya series in Pakistan, Musa ka Musalla (13,500 feet above sea level), and has a cool climate.

Mundi

Mundi is a beautiful tourist spot located in the Mansehra district of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province in Pakistan. It is known for its picturesque natural beauty and serene environment, making it a popular destination for tourists. Mundi is located at an elevation of about 7,000 feet above sea level
